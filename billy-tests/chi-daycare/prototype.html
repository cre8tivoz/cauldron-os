About Tiny Tyrants

Small dogs don’t need less care. They need the right kind.

Tiny Tyrants was built for chihuahuas who are clever, expressive, slightly bossy, and not always thrilled by bigger-dog energy. We keep routines structured, supervision attentive, and the mood calm, bright, and affectionate.

This is not generic dog day care with a tiny-dog corner added as an afterthought. It’s a boutique, welfare-led environment for little personalities who benefit from size-appropriate play, predictable rest, and handlers who understand that confidence and sensitivity often come in the same 2.8kg package.
